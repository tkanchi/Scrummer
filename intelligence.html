Risk Score (MVP Formula)

We’ll compute a 0–100 score from a few simple signals first (fast, reliable), then improve later.

Inputs: Committed SP, last 3 sprint velocities, sprint days, team members, planned leave/holidays.
Signals (shown above):
- Scope pressure = Committed ÷ Avg velocity
- Capacity shortfall = Committed ÷ Effective capacity (availability-adjusted)
- Focus = Effective capacity ÷ Avg velocity
Risk score (0–100): weighted blend of scope pressure, capacity shortfall, and volatility.
Confidence (%): starts from capacity vs commitment and is reduced by volatility.
